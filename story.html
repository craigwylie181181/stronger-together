Our story

It started with one Instagram post.

In 2024, Chloe Wilson asked if any women wanted to go for a run in Abu Dhabi. Three showed up on the Corniche at 6am. Two years later, Stronger Together is 6,000 women and counting — and now, an activewear brand.

The manifesto

We don't talk about community. We show up and be it.

Two years ago, there was no run club. There was just a woman, an Instagram story, and a quiet wish that someone else in Abu Dhabi wanted what she wanted — a 6am on the Corniche that felt like something.

Three women showed up that first Friday. Ten the next. Thirty by the end of the year. By the summer of 2025, there were three figures. By this April — our first drop — there are over 6,000.

We didn't run an ad. We didn't hire an influencer. We didn't optimise a funnel. We just kept showing up. Every Friday. Same time. Same place. Through Ramadan, through summer, through travel, through grief, through new jobs and new babies and new heartbreaks.

The brand is the second thing. The community is the first thing.

Stronger Together — the clothes — exists because we were already wearing each other's leggings. Borrowing each other's bras between sessions. Agreeing that the fabric we wanted didn't exist at the price we wanted it, and the colours we wanted didn't exist at all.

So we're making it. The way we do everything — inside the club, not marketed at it. Every sample tested at a real Friday run. Every colour chosen to look right against Gulf sand. Every price reviewed for what members can actually afford.

This is an activewear brand built by a community, for a community, owned in part by the community. It will never be the cheapest. It will always be honest. And the Friday run will outlive the brand. That's a promise.

The founder

Chloe Wilson.

A British expat, a cycling instructor at Crank Abu Dhabi, and the accidental founder of the UAE's largest women's run club. Chloe didn't set out to build a community. She set out to find three other women who wanted to run.

The product line is a natural extension — co-founded with Chloe, co-designed with a circle of club members, and co-owned so the community gets a real stake in what gets made and who gets hired.

"I didn't build a club. I just kept showing up at 6am. Everyone else built the club around me."

— Chloe Wilson, April 2026

The timeline

Two years. In ten moments.

Mar 2024

The Instagram post.

Chloe asks if anyone in Abu Dhabi wants to go for a run. Three women show up at the Corniche at 6am that Friday. Nobody takes a photo.

Jun 2024

The WhatsApp group.

20 regulars. The group chat forms. Someone creates the hashtag #strongertogether. It sticks.

Oct 2024

The Instagram account.

The club goes public. 50 runners show up the following Friday. The Corniche gets its first sunrise run club photograph.

Feb 2025

Khaleej Times feature.

First press coverage. Membership doubles in two weeks. The summer pause idea is debated and rejected — the club runs through 42°C.

Jul 2025

1,000 members.

Monday lift sessions added at Reem Central Park. First club coffee morning at Galleria.

Nov 2025

The apparel question.

At the Saadiyat Long Run, three members wear matching unbranded leggings from Shein. Someone says out loud: "we should just make our own". That sentence becomes the brand.

Jan 2026

First sample round.

A Portuguese factory agrees to run small batches. The Friday Legging v1 arrives in Abu Dhabi. 20 members test wear it for a month.

Feb 2026

Crank partnership.

The Crank Line is greenlit. Cycling-specific kit designed with Crank Abu Dhabi's women's ride leaders. First jerseys tested on Yas Island.

Mar 2026

6,000 members.

The club hits six thousand. The Instagram doesn't have 6,000 followers yet — those are just runners, in WhatsApp groups, coming on Fridays.

Apr 2026

The first drop.

Stronger Together launches as an activewear brand. 12 products. Three lines. One community. You're reading this on launch week.

What we stand for

Four things we won't negotiate.

01 · Community First

Every decision runs through one filter.

Does this serve the women in the community first? If not, we don't do it. Not a value we put on the website and forget. A filter we run every choice through.

02 · Desert-Born

UAE heat. UAE bodies. UAE life.

Warm neutrals. Earthy terracottas. Fabrics engineered for 42°C, not Vancouver yoga studios. Abu Dhabi is in the DNA of everything we make — it's not the backdrop, it's the product brief.

03 · Premium, Always

Lululemon and Gymshark quality tier.

78% Nylon 6,6. 22% Elastane. Minimum 220gsm. Four-way stretch, squat-proof, moisture-wick. Four to six sampling rounds before a single unit goes to production.

04 · All Abilities

We don't race. We run together.

Open to women of every fitness level. The brand doesn't tell you how fast to go. It shows up with you at 6am and sees you at the end, whenever that is.

This Friday

The clothing is optional. The coffee after is non-negotiable. Everyone you'll meet walked in on a Friday exactly like you will — as a stranger who just wanted to run.
