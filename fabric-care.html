Fabric & Care

Every piece in the collection is built to handle Abu Dhabi heat, high-intensity training, and weekly washes without losing shape, colour, or compression.

The Friday Line (Running)

Our signature 220gsm double-knit compression fabric. 78% recycled polyester, 22% elastane. Four-way stretch, squat-proof opacity, and UPF 40+ sun protection. The brushed inner face wicks moisture while the smooth outer resists pilling. Developed over six sample rounds at our mill in Porto, Portugal.

The Foundation (Gym)

A lighter 180gsm ribbed knit for studio and strength sessions. 72% recycled nylon, 28% elastane. Buttery soft hand-feel with recovery stretch. Breathable mesh panels in high-heat zones keep you cool under indoor lighting.

The Crank (Cycling)

Italian-milled 160gsm jersey fabric for the tops, 200gsm power lycra for the bib shorts. The jersey uses a honeycomb texture for airflow at speed, while the bib short features a women’s-specific multi-density chamois pad rated for 100km+ rides. Flatlock seams throughout to prevent chafing.

Care Instructions

High-performance fabrics last longer with a little extra care:

  • Machine wash cold (30°C max) on a gentle cycle
  • Turn garments inside out before washing
  • Use a mesh laundry bag — it protects fibres and elastane
  • Avoid fabric softener (it coats the fibres and kills moisture-wicking)
  • Hang dry or lay flat — never tumble dry
  • Do not iron directly on prints or logos
  • Wash dark and light colours separately for the first few washes

With proper care, our fabrics maintain compression and colour for 100+ washes. We tested them so you don’t have to wonder.
