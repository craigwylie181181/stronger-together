The positioning

The only women's activewear brand built inside a real community, not marketed at one.

6,000+ women show up at the Friday run. That number didn't come from a campaign. It came from one woman posting on Instagram asking if anyone wanted to come for a run. What happened next is the brand.

Founded by Chloe Wilson

From three women to six thousand.

In 2024, Chloe posted on Instagram asking if any women wanted to go for a run in Abu Dhabi. Three showed up. Two years later, the Stronger Together Run Club is 6,000 women deep, every Friday on the Corniche, growing every week.

The clothing brand isn't a pivot. It's the natural next step — a product line designed and tested inside the community that's already been wearing each other's workout wardrobes for two years. Co-founded with Chloe. Co-owned. Co-designed.

02 — What we stand for

Four things we won't negotiate.

01 · Community First

Every decision runs through one filter.

Does this serve the women in the community first? If not, we don't do it. Not a value we put on the website and forget. A filter we run every choice through.

02 · Desert-Born

UAE heat. UAE bodies. UAE life.

Warm neutrals. Earthy terracottas. Fabrics engineered for 42°C, not Vancouver yoga studios. Abu Dhabi is in the DNA of everything we make — it's not the backdrop, it's the product brief.

03 · Premium, Always

Lululemon and Gymshark quality tier.

78% Nylon 6,6. 22% Elastane. Minimum 220gsm. Four-way stretch, squat-proof, moisture-wick. Four to six sampling rounds before a single unit goes to production.

04 · All Abilities

We don't race. We run together.

Open to women of every fitness level. The brand doesn't tell you how fast to go. It shows up with you at 6am and sees you at the end, whenever that is.
