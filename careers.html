Careers

We’re a small, fast-moving team based in Abu Dhabi building a brand that belongs to its community. If you care about women’s sport, great product, and doing things differently — we’d like to hear from you.

How We Work

Our team is small by design. Everyone wears multiple hats. We value ownership, taste, and the ability to ship — over credentials and process. We work Sunday to Thursday from our studio in Abu Dhabi, with flexibility for remote days.

Open Roles

Abu Dhabi · Freelance
