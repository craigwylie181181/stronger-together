How to Measure

Use a soft tape measure against bare skin. Stand relaxed — don’t hold your breath. Measure your bust at the fullest point, waist at the narrowest point above the navel, and hips at the widest point. If you’re between sizes or unsure, email us at hello@strongertogether.ae — we’re happy to help you find your fit.
