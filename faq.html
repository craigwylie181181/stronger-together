Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering & Shipping

Do you ship outside the UAE?

Yes. We ship across the GCC — Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Bahrain, Oman, and Kuwait. International shipping beyond the GCC is coming later in 2026.

How long does delivery take?

UAE orders arrive in 1–3 business days. GCC orders take 3–6 business days. Orders placed before 2pm GST ship same day.

Can I track my order?

Yes — you’ll receive a tracking link via email and WhatsApp as soon as your order ships.

What payment methods do you accept?

Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay, and buy-now-pay-later through Tabby and Tamara (split into 4 interest-free payments).

Products & Sizing

Are your leggings squat-proof?

Yes. Our Friday Line leggings use 220gsm double-knit fabric specifically chosen for full opacity under stretch. We test every colourway.

What size should I order?

Our pieces fit true to size. Check our size guide for detailed measurements. If you’re between sizes, size up for a relaxed feel or stay true for compression.

Are your fabrics sustainable?

Our Friday and Foundation lines use recycled polyester and nylon. Our packaging is plastic-free. Read more on our sustainability page.

The Club

Is the Friday run really free?

Completely free. No membership, no sign-up fee, no catch. Show up at the Abu Dhabi Corniche at 5:45am on Friday and run with us. All paces welcome.

Do I need to buy your kit to join?

Absolutely not. Wear whatever you train in. The club and the brand are two sides of the same community, but one never requires the other.

Is it women only?

The Friday run and all Stronger Together club sessions are women-only spaces. It’s core to who we are and what makes the community feel safe and supportive.

Returns & Exchanges

What’s your return policy?

14 days from delivery, unworn with tags attached. Email returns@strongertogether.ae and we’ll send a prepaid label (UAE) within 24 hours. Full details on our shipping & returns page.

Can I exchange for a different size?

Yes. Email us and we’ll reserve your new size while the return is in transit — you won’t have to wait for the refund.
