Studio Partners

We built Stronger Together inside a real fitness community — so partnering with gyms, studios, and clubs is in our DNA. If you run a women’s fitness space in the UAE or GCC, we’d love to work together.

What We Offer

  • Wholesale pricing: Competitive rates on full-size runs for retail display or staff kitting
  • Co-branded events: Joint workout sessions, launch events, and community pop-ups
  • Exclusive colourways: For select partners, we can produce limited-edition pieces in your studio’s brand colours
  • Affiliate programme: Earn commission on sales driven through your studio with a unique referral link
  • Product seeding: We’ll kit out your instructors so your members can see the product in action
