How we show up

The only rules.

Four things the club agrees on, always, without exception.

  • 01Every ability. Walkers, 6-minute milers, post-partum, pre-race. Nobody gets left behind.
  • 02No photos of other members without asking. Ever.
  • 03Women only. This isn't negotiable. Male allies support from the sidelines.
  • 04If you're new, come. If you're slow, come. If you're nervous, come. That's the whole point.
