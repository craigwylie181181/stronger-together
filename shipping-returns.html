Returns

Not the right fit? We get it — activewear has to feel perfect. You have 14 days from delivery to return any unworn item with tags attached. Here’s how:

  • Email returns@strongertogether.ae with your order number
  • We’ll send a prepaid return label within 24 hours (UAE only; GCC returns ship at customer cost)
  • Drop off at any Emirates Post or Aramex counter
  • Refunds are processed within 5 business days of receiving the return

Sale items and intimates (sports bras with hygiene seal removed) are final sale for hygiene reasons.

Exchanges

Need a different size? Email us and we’ll reserve your new size while the return is in transit — no waiting for the refund to clear first.
