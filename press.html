Stronger Together is a women’s activewear brand and running community born in Abu Dhabi. Founded in 2026, the brand grew out of a free Friday morning run club that now counts over 6,000 members across the UAE.

The Story

What started as three women running the Corniche at 6am became Abu Dhabi’s largest women-only fitness community. When members couldn’t find activewear that survived Gulf heat, looked good on every body, and didn’t cost a week’s salary, founder Chloe decided to make it herself — with the community as co-designers and shareholders.

The brand’s three product lines (Friday for running, Foundation for gym, and Crank for cycling) are all designed in Abu Dhabi and manufactured in Porto, Portugal using recycled performance fabrics.

Key Facts

  • Founded: 2026, Abu Dhabi, UAE
  • Community: 6,000+ women across the UAE
  • Product lines: The Friday Line (running), The Foundation (gym), The Crank (cycling)
  • Manufacturing: Porto, Portugal — recycled performance fabrics
  • Distribution: UAE and GCC (Saudi, Qatar, Bahrain, Oman, Kuwait)
  • Club: Free weekly sessions — running, gym, and cycling. Women only.
